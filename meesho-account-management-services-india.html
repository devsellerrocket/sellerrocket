How to Become a Seller on Meesho? (A Complete 2024 Guide)

Do you want to sell your products on Meesho and reach crores of customers across India? With its 0% commission policy, Meesho offers a fantastic opportunity for small and medium businesses. In this complete guide, you'll learn how to easily create a Meesho Seller Account, what documents you need, and how to list your very first product for sale.

Why Sell on Meesho? (Benefits of Selling on Meesho)

  • 0% Commission: Unlike other online marketplaces, you don't have to share your profits as commission on Meesho.
  • Crores of Customers: You can sell your products to over 11 crore customers across India.
  • Minimal Investment: Start your online store for free, with no registration fees.
  • Easy Shipping: Easily deliver your products to 28,000+ pincodes across India through Meesho's logistics partners.
  • Timely Payments: Payments are securely deposited into your bank account within 7 days of your product's delivery.

Requirements to Start Selling on Meesho

Before you begin the registration process, make sure you have the following documents ready:

  • GST Number (GSTIN): A GSTIN is mandatory to sell products online in India. The only exception is if you sell goods that are exempt from GST.
  • PAN Card: Your business or individual PAN card is required.
  • Bank Account: You will need an active current or savings bank account in your name or your business's name.

How to List Your First Product (Catalog Upload)

  1. Log in to the Meesho Supplier Panel.
  2. From the menu on the left, select "Catalog Upload."
  3. Choose "Add New Catalog in Bulk" and select the category for your product.
  4. Download the Excel template and fill in your product details.
  5. Upload clear images of your products, preferably with a white background.
  6. Upload the filled Excel file and click "Submit."

If uploaded correctly, your products will go live on Meesho within a few hours after a quality check.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I sell on Meesho without GST?

A GST number is mandatory for all products, except for certain categories that are exempt from GST, such as books.

When will I receive my payment from Meesho?

Payments are credited to your bank account on the 7th day from the date of the product's delivery to the customer.

PWhat should I do with returned products on Meesho?

If a returned product is undamaged, you can resell it. If it is damaged, you can file a claim for reimbursement.
