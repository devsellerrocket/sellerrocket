Maximize Your Profits: Proven Strategies for Selling on Walmart Marketplace

By Kannan Palaniyappan Business Models 12 Comments

Amazon Seller Account Free Audit | Boost your online sales Amazon PPC Campaign

Fellow Amazon Seller, Are you looking to boost sales while cutting down on the ROAS? Your opportunity on the platform is only as good as your Amazon seller account free audit. With a little assessment, implementing some strategic changes can ramp up those sales and cut costs as well, making your overall experience of selling on Amazon much better.

What is an Amazon Seller Account Audit?

In simple terms, an Amazon seller account audit is a thorough analysis of your seller account health, performance, and compliance with the platform’s policies. This encompasses an in-depth look at different areas of your account, such as:

Product Listings: Breakdown of titles, bullet points, images and A+ content

PRICING STRATEGY: Status of pricing competitiveness and profitability

Pay Per Click Ads Campaigns: Analysis of PPC campaigns, manage cost-per-click bids and targeting

Inventory Management: Analysis of inventory levels, fulfillment options and storage fees.

Customer Reviews and Feedback: Analysis of customer mood and response tactics

Account Health: Comprehensive review of policy compliance, performance metrics, and any open cases

Why Should You Consider an Amazon Seller Account Audit?

There are plenty of benefits you can get from audit your amazon seller account. Some positive aspects, include:

Higher Sales: By utilizing search engine optimization for product listings, devising a pricing method or advertising strategy, you can boost buyer attention and/or sales.

Higher Profitability: Cutting costs and performing processes efficiently will increase your margins.

Improved Brand Reputation: A commitment to outstanding service and providing timely solutions to customer concerns can greatly boost your brand image.

Better Account Health: Remaining compliant with Amazon policies can help you prevent suspensions and other disciplinary actions against your account.

Make your decisions data-based — You have the account data which enables you to make the right driving business decisions based on it.

Key Areas to Focus on During an Amazon Seller Account Audit

1. Product Listings:

o Keyword Optimization: Ensure your product titles, descriptions, and backend search terms are optimized for relevant keywords.

o High-Quality Images: Use clear, high-resolution images that showcase your products effectively.

o A+ Content: Create compelling A+ content to highlight your product’s features and benefits.

o Customer Reviews: Monitor customer reviews and respond promptly to both positive and negative feedback.

2. Pricing Strategy:

o Competitive Pricing: Analyze your competitors’ pricing and adjust your prices accordingly.

o Promotional Strategies: Utilize Amazon’s promotional tools, such as coupons and discounts, to attract customers.

o Dynamic Pricing: Consider using dynamic pricing strategies to optimize your pricing based on demand and competition.

3. Advertising Campaigns:

o Campaign Structure: Ensure your campaigns are well-organized and targeted.

o Keyword Optimization: Use relevant keywords to attract the right audience.

o Bid Management: Optimize your bids to maximize your ROI.

o Ad Copy:Write compelling ad copy to entice clicks.

4. Inventory Management:

o Stock Levels: Maintain optimal stock levels to avoid stockouts and excess inventory.

o Fulfillment Strategy: Choose the right fulfillment method (FBA or FBM) to meet your business needs.

o Storage Fees: Minimize storage fees by optimizing your inventory levels and using storage limits effectively.

5. Customer Service:

o Response Time: Respond to customer inquiries and messages promptly.

o Problem Resolution: Address customer issues and concerns efficiently.

o Positive Customer Experience: Strive to provide excellent customer service to build brand loyalty.
